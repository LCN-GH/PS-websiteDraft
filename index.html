Hello World — It's Me

Welcome to my brand new website (formerly www.petinastrohmer.com) I am a writer (and smallholder and teacher and...) currently living in the beautiful Brecon Beacons of South Wales.

About me    

  • My first novel, "Truly Blue; A Rock & Roll Parable" was published by Leaf Books (www.leafbooks.co.uk) in March 2009.

  • My second novel,"Entertaining Angels" was published by Cinnamon Press (www.cinnamonpress.com) in May 2016

  • I also write short stories, plays, magazine articles, educational resources and the occasional biography. I am not, however, as adept with computers (yet) so please bear with me as I put this new website together.

Latest News

  • (May 2018): My third novel, "Gifted", is currently in competition at The Ink and Insights Novel Competition, The Bath Novel Award and The Yeovil Literary Prize.

  • To celebrate the centenary of the end of WW1, I have added my grandfather's timely memoir on the page "No Ordinary Man"
